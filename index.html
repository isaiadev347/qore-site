QORE

NTU Control Plane for AI compute.

QORE is software for operators and teams that need transparent AI runtime pricing, routing diagnostics, and NTU‑based cost measurement across multiple providers and models.

What QORE Does

Software and Services

QORE provides software and services for:

  • Viewing normalized AI compute pricing across providers using an internal unit (NTU).
  • Inspecting routing behavior and basic diagnostics for AI workloads.
  • Measuring and reporting AI runtime economics to operators and finance teams.

QORE is not a token, cryptocurrency, or financial instrument. NTU is an internal measurement unit used only inside the software to compare AI compute across providers and workloads.
